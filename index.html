Short information

GetGrass.io is a platform where users can earn money passively by sharing their unused internet bandwidth. Users need an invitation code to join, and the platform works as a Chrome extension, making it easy to install and use. Earnings depend on the user's country and connection, with users earning Grass points that may have future benefits as the platform develops. While the platform mentions exploring payment methods like cryptocurrency, gift cards, and PayPal, there are currently no guaranteed payout options. Users are advised to approach GetGrass.io with caution due to uncertainties about the value of points, payout methods, and when payouts will be available. The platform is in beta, so users may encounter bugs or changes during testing.

5IRE

→ Main page ←

Short information

5ire is a blockchain project that aims to merge blockchain technology with sustainability, focusing on the transition to the fifth industrial revolution (5IR). The project offers no-code smart contracts that are faster and cheaper than existing blockchains, allowing interoperability to send assets, collectibles, or files across various platforms. It emphasizes decentralization with user-owned wallets hosted on web3 domains and boasts a zero-carbon footprint, aligning with sustainable practices. The ultimate goal of 5ire is to make blockchain technology accessible and sustainable for over 1 billion people by 2030, leading the transition from the fourth industrial revolution (4IR) to the human-centered designs of 5IR.

BTT

→ Main page ←

Short information

BitTorrent Token (BTT) is a utility token based on the Tron blockchain, designed to enhance the BitTorrent protocol. BTT was created to incentivize users to seed files longer and dedicate more bandwidth and storage to the network. It is used within the BitTorrent ecosystem for various purposes, such as powering the BitTorrent Speed feature, which allows faster download speeds and token rewards, and the BitTorrent File System (BTFS), a decentralized file storage system supported by millions of BitTorrent user nodes. BTT is also integrated into data storage devices like Seagate hard drives. The token has a market capitalization of $685 million and is available on several cryptocurrency exchanges, including Binance and KuCoin.
